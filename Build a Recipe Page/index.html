Chocolate Chip Cookies

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making mini chocolate chip cookies! These bite-sized treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without overindulging.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter, sugar, and vanilla until creamy.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into wet mixture, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
  6. Bake 8–10 minutes, then cool.
